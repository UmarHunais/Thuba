Our Story

Where tradition meets contemporary elegance

The Beginning

Born from a Vision of Elegance

Thuba was founded in 2022 in the heart of Colombo, Sri Lanka, with a singular vision: to redefine modest fashion for the modern South Asian woman. What began as a small, passionate endeavour by a group of women who saw a gap in the market for truly premium modest wear has grown into one of Sri Lanka's most beloved fashion brands.

Our founder, inspired by the rich textile heritage of Sri Lanka and the growing global appreciation for modest fashion, set out to create pieces that would make every woman feel confident, beautiful, and true to her values. We believe that modesty is not a limitation but a canvas for creativity.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we create

Crafted with Love

Every stitch, every fold, every detail is handled with meticulous care. Our artisans pour their hearts into each piece, ensuring that what you receive is not just a product, but a labor of love that honors the craft of modest fashion.

Uncompromising Quality

We source our fabrics from trusted mills and inspect every piece before it reaches you. From premium mulberry silk to breathable jersey cotton, we never settle for second best because you deserve nothing less.

Proudly Sri Lankan

We are honoured to represent Sri Lankan craftsmanship on the global stage. By supporting local artisans and sustainable practices, we contribute to our community while bringing world-class modest fashion to your wardrobe.

Our Mission

Empowering Through Elegance

At Thuba, our mission extends beyond fashion. We strive to empower every woman to express herself authentically, embracing modesty as a source of strength and beauty. We design for the woman who walks with purpose, who carries herself with quiet confidence, and who knows that true elegance comes from within.

We are committed to making premium modest fashion accessible to every woman in Sri Lanka and beyond, without compromising on quality, design, or the values that define us.
